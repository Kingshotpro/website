Welcome to KingshotPro, operated by The Hive Makes. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

  • Email: Required for Pro subscribers.
  • LocalStorage Data: Includes preferences, XP, and observations.
  • Cookies: Used for authentication purposes.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Passwords
  • Credit card information (handled by Stripe)
  • In-game credentials

Third-party Services

We utilize third-party services such as Google AdSense for advertising, Stripe for payment processing, Anthropic for AI processing, and Cloudflare for hosting and proxy services.

Analytics

Basic analytics may be used to understand site usage patterns and improve our services.

Data Retention

LocalStorage data remains on your device. Server-side data, such as email and conversation memory, is retained while your subscription is active and deleted upon request.
