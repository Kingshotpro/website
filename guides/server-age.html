Server Age Guide: What Happens at 90, 180, and 360 Days

As a seasoned player in Kingshot, understanding the evolution of your server is crucial to your success. Each server in Kingshot undergoes significant changes as it ages, and being aware of these milestones can help you strategize effectively.

90 Days: The First Milestone

At 90 days, the server reaches its first major milestone. By this time, most players have established their kingdoms, and alliances begin to solidify. This is a critical period for new players to find a strong alliance, as the power dynamics start to stabilize. Migration options are typically not available yet, so players must focus on local growth and alliances.

180 Days: Mid-Game Dynamics

At the 180-day mark, the server enters its mid-game phase. Alliances have formed strong bonds, and the competition for resources intensifies. This is when migration might open, allowing players to move to younger servers if they wish. KvK (Kingdom vs Kingdom) matchmaking becomes more challenging as alliances strategize for dominance. For new players, joining an established server at this stage can be daunting, but it also offers opportunities to learn from experienced players.

360 Days: The Veteran Stage

By the time a server hits 360 days, it is considered a veteran server. The top alliances have likely consolidated their power, and the server's political landscape is well-defined. Migration options are more flexible, allowing players to seek new challenges or reinforce their current positions. KvK battles are intense and require strategic planning. For old players on a new server, this is a chance to leverage their experience and guide newer players.

New Players on Old Servers vs. Old Players on New Servers

Joining an old server as a new player can be challenging due to established power structures, but it offers a wealth of knowledge and the potential to rise quickly by aligning with the right allies. Conversely, old players on new servers can dominate early on, using their experience to guide new alliances and shape the server's future.

Understanding these server age dynamics is key to thriving in Kingshot. Adapt your strategies to the server's age, and you'll find success in the ever-evolving world of Kingshot.
