Furnace Guide: When to Upgrade and Why It Matters

Understanding the Furnace

The furnace is a crucial building in KingshotPro, serving as the heart of your gear crafting operations. It allows you to smelt raw materials into usable components for crafting equipment. Upgrading your furnace not only increases its efficiency but also unlocks higher-tier gear, essential for advancing through the game.

Why the Furnace Gates Your Progression

In KingshotPro, progression is heavily tied to the quality of your gear. As you face tougher opponents and more challenging quests, having advanced gear becomes indispensable. The furnace gates this progression by determining the maximum tier of gear you can craft. Without timely upgrades, you might find yourself stuck against formidable foes.

When to Prioritize the Furnace Over Other Buildings

While upgrading various buildings is important, the furnace should be a priority when your current gear is insufficient for your level. If you notice that battles are becoming increasingly difficult, it might be time to focus resources on your furnace. However, balance is key; ensure your resource production and storage facilities are also adequate to support these upgrades.

Gear Crafting Basics

Crafting gear involves several steps: collecting raw materials, refining them in the furnace, and then using them to craft equipment. Each tier of gear requires specific materials and a certain level of furnace upgrade. Always keep an eye on the materials needed for the next tier of gear to plan your upgrades efficiently.

Furnace Milestones and Major Gear Tiers

Reaching certain furnace levels unlocks major gear tiers, significantly boosting your combat effectiveness. For instance, upgrading your furnace to level 5 might unlock the ability to craft rare gear, while level 10 could allow for legendary gear crafting. Prioritize these milestones to ensure your gear remains competitive.

Written by an experienced KingshotPro player, this guide provides practical advice on optimizing your furnace upgrades for maximum progression.
