Kingshot Beginner Guide: Your First 30 Days

Welcome + What This Guide Covers

Welcome to the ultimate beginner's guide for Kingshot, the thrilling mobile strategy game by Century Games. This guide is crafted to navigate you through your first 30 days, ensuring a solid foundation for growth and success. Whether you're aiming to become a formidable force or simply enjoy the strategic depth of the game, this guide will cover everything you need to know. From the critical early days to mid-game transitions, we will delve into building strategies, research priorities, gem spending, troop training, hero gear optimization, event participation, and common pitfalls to avoid.

Each section is designed to provide you with actionable insights and tips, ensuring that you maximize your resources and time. By following this guide, you'll be well-equipped to make informed decisions that align with your long-term goals in Kingshot. Let's dive in and set you on the path to becoming a master strategist!

Day 1-3: The Critical Start

The first few days in Kingshot are crucial for setting the pace of your development. Your primary objective should be to rush your Town Center to level 5. This unlocks additional features and buildings that are essential for rapid growth. Focus on completing chapter missions as they provide valuable rewards and guide your initial progression. These missions often coincide with necessary building upgrades, making them a perfect roadmap for your early days.

One of the most critical early investments is acquiring a second building queue. This allows you to construct or upgrade two buildings simultaneously, significantly accelerating your progress. Use gems wisely to unlock this feature as soon as possible. Additionally, prioritize upgrading resource buildings to ensure a steady flow of materials needed for continuous development.

Your First Week: Research and Alliance

Research is the backbone of your kingdom's development. During your first week, focus on economic research to boost resource production and construction speed. These benefits compound over time, providing an essential advantage as you progress. Key research areas include Construction Speed, Resource Production, and Gathering Speed. Prioritizing these will ensure that your kingdom's growth is sustainable.

Joining an alliance is another critical step. Alliances offer numerous benefits, including help with construction and research, access to alliance events, and protection from potential threats. Use the alliance help feature to reduce timers on your builds and research, effectively speeding up your progress. Engage actively with your alliance members to maximize these benefits and participate in group activities for additional rewards.

Gems: How to Spend Them

Gems are a valuable currency in Kingshot and should be spent wisely. Your first priority should be unlocking the second building queue, as mentioned earlier. This provides a massive boost to your development speed. After securing the second queue, consider investing in VIP levels. Higher VIP levels offer various perks, such as increased construction speed and additional daily rewards.

Avoid spending gems on resources or speed-ups unless absolutely necessary. These can be acquired through other means, and spending gems on them can quickly deplete your reserves. Instead, focus on long-term investments that enhance your overall gameplay experience and efficiency.

Troops and Training

Understanding the troop tiers and when to unlock them is crucial for maintaining a strong military presence. Start by training T1 troops to gather resources and complete early game objectives. As you progress, unlock T2 and T3 troops to bolster your army's strength. Aim to unlock T4 troops by the end of your first month, as they are significantly more powerful and versatile.

Participate in training events whenever possible to maximize rewards and efficiency. These events often provide resources and speed-ups, which are essential for maintaining a steady troop production. Balance your troop training with resource availability to ensure you are not depleting your reserves too quickly.

Hero Gear: Your Hidden Multiplier

Hero gear is a powerful tool that can significantly enhance your army's capabilities. Focus on using the furnace to melt down excess gear and craft new items. Prioritize crafting gear that boosts your tank loadout, as these heroes often lead your armies and benefit the most from enhanced stats.

As you progress, aim to collect sets of gear that provide additional bonuses when equipped together. These set bonuses can provide substantial boosts to your hero's effectiveness, making them invaluable in both PvE and PvP scenarios. Regularly upgrade your gear to keep pace with your evolving strategy and ensure your heroes remain competitive.

Events: Where F2P Players Win

Events are a fantastic opportunity for free-to-play (F2P) players to gain resources, speed-ups, and rare items. Focus on completing growth missions and participating in the Hall of Governors to earn valuable rewards. These events often align with regular gameplay activities, making them a natural fit for your daily routine.

Save your speed-ups for event milestones to maximize your rewards. Timing is crucial, and using speed-ups strategically during events can propel you to higher reward tiers. Keep an eye on the event calendar and plan your activities accordingly to ensure you don't miss out on these opportunities.

Week 2-4: Mid-Game Transition

As you transition into the mid-game, it's time to start specializing in certain areas of gameplay. Whether it's focusing on military strength, economic prowess, or alliance support, specialization will help you carve out a niche and contribute more effectively to your alliance. Communicate with your alliance to determine the best specialization based on your team's needs.

Engage in alliance wars and coordinate with your teammates to maximize your impact. Resource management becomes increasingly important during this phase, as you'll need to balance growth with military expenditures. Focus on gathering and protecting resources to maintain a steady supply for your kingdom's needs.

Common Mistakes

Avoid the common mistake of spreading yourself too thin. Focus your efforts on a few key areas rather than trying to excel in everything at once. This approach will yield better results and prevent resource wastage. Another common error is wasting gems on unnecessary purchases. Always prioritize long-term benefits over short-term gains.

Ignoring research is another pitfall to avoid. Research provides critical benefits that enhance all aspects of gameplay. Make it a habit to always have research ongoing. Lastly, avoid going solo. The game is designed to be played with others, and joining an alliance provides numerous advantages that solo players miss out on.

Summary Checklist

  • Rush Town Center to level 5 in the first 3 days.
  • Acquire a second building queue with gems.
  • Focus on economic research in the first week.
  • Join an active alliance and use the help feature.
  • Unlock T4 troops by the end of the first month.
  • Craft and upgrade hero gear for tank loadouts.
  • Participate in events for F2P rewards.
  • Specialize in a gameplay area during weeks 2-4.
  • Avoid spreading resources too thin and wasting gems.

By following this guide, you'll establish a strong foundation for success in Kingshot. Remember, strategic planning and alliance cooperation are key to becoming a formidable player. Good luck, and enjoy the journey!
