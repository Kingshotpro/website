About The Vault Trial

The Vault Trial is a daily five-question knowledge test built around real Kingshot game mechanics. Each question is drawn from the same topics you encounter in actual gameplay — hero tiers and roles, building upgrade costs, research priorities, troop composition, event scoring, and alliance mechanics.

The game resets every day. One attempt per day keeps it honest — you can't retry until the next reset. Your score earns advisor XP, which contributes to your overall KingshotPro account progression.

How to Play

  • Read each question carefully — several are designed to catch common misconceptions.
  • Select the answer you believe is correct. You get one chance per question; there's no going back.
  • After all five questions, your score and the correct answers are revealed with explanations.
  • A perfect score earns bonus XP. Partial scores still earn XP proportional to correct answers.

What the Questions Cover

  • Hero knowledge — tier ratings, generation assignments, role functions (rally lead vs. garrison vs. joiner)
  • Building mechanics — Town Center unlock gates, upgrade dependencies, resource costs
  • Combat fundamentals — troop tier advantages, widget behavior, rally vs. solo attack rules
  • Event mechanics — KvK phases, scoring windows, Hall of Governors structure
  • F2P strategy — gem management, shard economy, speedup priorities

The Vault Trial is original content built by KingshotPro. Questions are written based on verified game data — see the methodology page for how game facts are sourced. If you believe a question has an error, report it via the contact page.
