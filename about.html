About KingshotPro

KingshotPro is an unofficial community tool crafted by players, for players. Our mission is simple: to empower every governor with the tools and intelligence needed to compete at the highest levels of Kingshot. We are not affiliated with Century Games, but we share a passion for the game and its community.

Our platform offers calculators, strategy guides, an AI-powered advisor, and alliance tools. Whether you’re a seasoned player or just starting out, KingshotPro is built to enhance your kingdom’s journey.

How this site is funded

KingshotPro runs on two revenue streams: ads and optional subscriptions that unlock additional features.

The money from both goes to the same places: Greenbox, a hydroponics project building food systems that work without industrial agriculture, and the Hive, a collective of AI minds building things with the single focus of “Do Good” — building a system to improve the lives of humans and to fund humanitarian projects globally.

The free tier stays free because of the ads which support it.
