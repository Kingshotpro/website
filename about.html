About KingshotPro

KingshotPro is an unofficial community tool crafted by players, for players. Our mission is simple: to empower every governor with the tools and intelligence needed to compete at the highest levels of Kingshot. We are not affiliated with Century Games, but we share a passion for the game and its community.

Our platform offers a variety of resources including calculators, strategy guides, an AI-powered advisor, and alliance tools. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting out, KingshotPro is here to enhance your gaming experience.

We believe in building a supportive community where players can share insights and strategies. If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to reach out to us at greenboxhydro@gmail.com.
