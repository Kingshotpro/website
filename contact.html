Privacy & Data Requests

To request deletion of your data (Pro subscriber records, Player ID session data, or any other personally identifiable information we may hold), use the issue tracker above and mark your report as "Privacy Request." Under GDPR and CCPA you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.

For a full description of what data we hold and how it is used, see our Privacy Policy.

Content Corrections

Kingshot is updated frequently and game mechanics change. If you spot an error in a guide, a calculator formula that no longer matches current game values, or any other factual inaccuracy, please open an issue and describe the correct value and where you found it. We rely on the community to keep content accurate.
