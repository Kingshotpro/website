Tips for Kingdom Transfer

  • Check In-Game Rules: Before you even think about transferring, go to the "Migrate" or "Transfer" section in your game settings to see the exact current requirements and any ongoing events that might affect them.
  • Save Passes Strategically: Transfer passes are valuable. Start saving them well in advance of your planned migration. Participate in events that offer them as rewards.
  • Alliance Coordination: If you're moving with your alliance or to join one, coordinate with them. There might be specific target kingdoms or power caps for incoming players.
  • Empty Resources: Before transferring, make sure your storage is below its protected limit, or send excess resources to your alliance warehouse or a farm account. You don't want to lose resources to tax or capacity limits during migration.
  • Unbind Troops: Ensure all your troops are in your city or gathering. Troops on rallies or occupying structures may prevent migration.
  • Power Cap: Be aware that some kingdoms have a power cap for incoming players, especially newer kingdoms or those under specific event rules. You might need to adjust your power (e.g., heal troops, remove equipment) to fit.
  • Cool-down Period: After a successful transfer, there's usually a cool-down period before you can transfer again. Plan your moves carefully.

Moving kingdoms can revitalize your game experience, offering new challenges, alliances, and opportunities. Use this calculator as a preliminary guide, but always prioritize official in-game information for your final plans!
