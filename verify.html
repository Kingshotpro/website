Verify Your Account

Prove you own your Kingshot account. Unlocks full advisor features and ties your subscription to your player.

Step 1: Request Verification

Enter your Player ID, kingdom number, and email. We will send a verification code to you in-game via mail within 24 hours.

Step 2: Enter Your Code

Check your in-game mail. Enter the 6-digit code you received.

How it works:
1. You submit your Player ID and kingdom.
2. A real person from our team finds you in-game and sends you a mail with a code.
3. You come back here and enter that code.
4. Your account is verified — your advisor knows you are who you say you are.

Who can verify:
• Pro, War Council, and Elite subscribers — verification included in your subscription (unlimited characters).
• Free users — $4.99 one-time fee per character. Or subscribe for unlimited verifications.

Multiple characters? Each character (Player ID) needs its own verification. Subscribers can verify as many as they want. Free users pay $4.99 per character.
How long: Codes are sent within 24 hours. The code expires after 48 hours.
